INGREDIENTS

  • Ground beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic Cloves
  • Red Wine
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Dried Thyme
  • Sugar
  • Parsley
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cottage cheese
  • Rocotta Cheese
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Eggs

    • Steps

    1. Add oil to a pan, then sautee onions and beef. After 5 minutes, add garlic add stir for 1 minute.

    2. Add 1/4 cup of wine then stir until it evaporates. Add marina,salt,thyme, pepper, sugar, and parsley.Cover and cook the tomatoe sause 5 minutes.

    3. Combine all the cheese sause ingredients together and 1 cup od mozzarella in a mixing bowl.

    4. Preheat your oven to 375 degrees fahrenheit. Cook the lasagna noodles in a pot with salt until al dente. Then drain the water out of the noodles and pour cold water onto them to prevent stickiness.

    5. Spread 1/2 cup of the meat sauce on the bottom of a 9(13)-inch casserole dish.

    6. Add 3 nmoodles, followedby 1/3 of the meat sauce, 1 cup of mozzarella and a half of ricotta.Repeat this by adding 3 more noodles and 1/3 of meat and the mozzarella remaining.

    7. Cover with aluminium foil and bake in the oven for 45 minutes , then remove the foil and boil for 3 to 5 minutes. Let the lasagna rest for 309 minutes before cutting and serving.
