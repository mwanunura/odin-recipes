INGREDIENTS

Chicken salad Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Celery
  • Grapes
  • Pecans
  • Red Onion

    • Chicken Salad Dressing

  • Mayonnaise
  • Sour Cream
  • Lemon juice
  • Dill

STEPS

  1. Place them on a dry skillet on low heat and toss them until they are golden. Transfer to a cutting board to coarsely chop and set aside for them to cool down and then put them in a large bowl.

  2. Dice cold chicken breasts and add it to the mixing bowl.

  3. Cut some grapes in half and add to the bowl.

  4. Cut celery sticks in half then slice and add in the bowl with the choped onions.

  5. Combine the dressing ingredients in a dish and then add dressing to the salad.

  6. Stir it until the salad is coated in dressing. Cover refrigerate until it's ready to serve.
