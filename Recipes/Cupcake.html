INGREDIENTS

  • 12/3 cups of cake flour
  • 1 tsp of baking powder
  • 1/2 tea spoon of baking soda
  • 1/2 tea spoon of salt
  • 6 tablespoons of unsalted butter, cut into 6-8 pieces[room temperature]
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 large egg white
  • 1/2 table spoon of vanilla extract
  • 1 cup of plain Greek Yogurt

FROSTING INGERDIENTS

  • 1 stick of unsalted butter
  • 11/2 cups of powdered sugar
  • 8 oz [1 stick] cream cheese,cut into 8 pieces
  • 1/2 cup about 15 of blackberries to puree plus blackberries for garnish

    • STEPS

    1. In a bowl that has an electirc mixer put the dry ingredients: 12/3 cups of cake flour, 1 cup of granulated sugar, 1 teaspoon of baking powder, 1/2 teaspoon od baking soda and 1/2 teaspoon of salt.Whisk well until it's well combined.
    2. Add 6 tablespoons of softened buteer with your whisk on low speed. Mix for 3-4 minutes.
    3. Then in a seperate bowl add the wet ingredients: 1 egg, 1 large egg white, 1 cup plain greek yoqurt and 1/2 tablespoons of vanilla extract and then whisk.

    4. Add the wet ingredients with the dry ingredients together and whisk at a medium speed. Put the cupcakes on paper line. Bake them at 350 derees fahrenheit for 18 -20 minutes. When it's done let it cool down at a room temperature.

    Make Blackberry Frosting

    1. Pure blackberries in small food processor, then strain them through a fine mesh Strainer. Discard seeds and set the pure aside.
    2. In a bowl of electric strand mixer, mix the 1 stick of softened butter with 11/2 cups powdered sugar and salt on low speed.[WARNING: Do not start from high speed].
    3. Once the mixture is combined , switch to medium speed and to high. Then beat until the cream is pale and fluffy
    4. Add cheese cream 1-piece and mix until it's combined.Beat until it is fluffly ,scrap the bowl if necessary. If you see globs , keep mixing.
    5. Pour in your blackberry puree and continue mixing until cooperated. Refregerate frosting for 10 minutes before piping on the cupcakes.
